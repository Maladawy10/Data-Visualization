without_zeros = df.query('budget > 0')
mean=without_zeros['budget'].mean()
low = df.query('budget < {}'.format(mean))
high = df.query('budget >= {}'.format(mean))
mean_low = low['popularity'].mean()
mean_high = high['popularity'].mean()
locations = [1, 2]
heights = [mean_low, mean_high]
labels = ['Low budget', 'High budget']
plt.bar(locations, heights, tick_label=labels)
plt.title('Average Popularity by Budget')
plt.xlabel('Budget')
plt.ylabel('Average Popularity');

Observations and Findings(code cell no.7)¶

low budget movies have pobularity less than high budget movies
